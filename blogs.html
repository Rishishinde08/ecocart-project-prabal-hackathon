Carbon Neutral Shipping

Etsy, a global online marketplace for handmade, vintage, and unique items, faced a major sustainability challenge by 2019: approximately 98% of its carbon footprint came from shipping emissions, despite not handling logistics directly. To address this, Etsy became the first major e-commerce platform to offer carbon-neutral shipping at scale by partnering with carbon offset provider 3Degrees. They invested in verified environmental projects, including forest conservation in Minnesota, renewable energy in India, and landfill gas capture. Crucially, Etsy covered all costs, requiring no changes from sellers or buyers. This initiative not only ensured 100% of Etsy orders shipped carbon-neutral from 2019 onward but also strengthened its reputation as a sustainability leader, built consumer trust, and set a powerful example for the broader e-commerce industry.
