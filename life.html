Complete Product Assessment

EcoCart evaluates every stage of a product's life cycle — from raw materials to disposal — so you can shop sustainably with confidence.

🧱 Raw Materials

Tracks sourcing impact—renewable vs. non-renewable, recycled content, and eco-certifications like FSC or GOTS.

learn more    →

🏭 Manufacturing

Analyzes energy use, chemical pollution, and waste during production. Highlights clean manufacturing practices.

learn more    →

📦 Packaging

Evaluates packaging types—biodegradable, recyclable, minimal. Encourages brands to reduce packaging waste.

learn more    →

🚚 Transportation

Measures carbon footprint from logistics based on distance, shipping mode, and fuel type.

learn more    →

⚡ Product Usage

Estimates energy or water used while using the product. Rates low-impact products like energy-saving appliances.

learn more    →

♻️ End-of-Life

Shows if the product is recyclable, reusable, compostable, or landfill-bound. Offers smart disposal suggestions.

learn more    →
