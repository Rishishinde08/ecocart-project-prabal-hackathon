HOW CAN WE HELP?

Welcome to the EcoCart Help Center! Here you can find answers to frequently asked questions, contact support, and access our community forums.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is EcoCart?
EcoCart is a sustainable shopping assistant that helps you make eco-friendly purchasing decisions. We analyze products and provide sustainability scores, tips, and better alternatives to reduce your environmental impact.
How does EcoCart calculate sustainability scores?
We evaluate products based on criteria like carbon footprint, packaging materials, sourcing practices, and brand transparency. Our scores are backed by data from trusted environmental sources.
Does using EcoCart guarantee Iâ€™m shopping sustainably?
While EcoCart guides you toward greener options, we recommend considering multiple aspects like local sourcing, minimal packaging, and personal consumption habits for a truly sustainable lifestyle.
Can I use EcoCart on any shopping website?
EcoCart works best with selected major retailers. It is a plugin that integrates with popular e-commerce platforms. You can check our website for a list of supported retailers.
Is my data safe with EcoCart?
Absolutely. We prioritize user privacy and never sell your data. Check out our [Privacy Policy] for full details.
Who created EcoCart?
EcoCart was developed as part of a student-led initiative during a hackathon, aimed at making sustainability easier and more accessible for everyone. We are a team of passionate individuals committed to promoting eco-friendly practices and reducing carbon footprints.
